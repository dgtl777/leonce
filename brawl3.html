Brawl Stars

Brawl Stars

This page is a short tutorial of how to play Brawl Stars:

-To move your basic character, drag the joystick at the bottom left of your screen in the direction you want your brawler(character) to go.

-To attack, drag the joystick at the bottom right of your screen to aim, or just tap it and it will automatically shoot at the nearest damageable thing.

On the start screen, select the button on the bottom right of your screen labeled "play" to enter a match.

Once you unlock them, you press the long rectangular button at the bottom of the screen to select a new event.

-To see your trophies and collect your trophy road rewards, select the other long rectangular button at the top of your screen, the one which says '_____ trophies'.

-To select another brawler, press the small button on the right of your screen with faces on and then tap on the brawler you want and press select. Similarly, to upgrade a brawler, just press on the same button, then press on the brawler you want, then press on the stats and press 'Upgrade'.

-To go onto the shop, just press on the button above the brawler button.

-To see the news, press on the button underneath the brawler button.

-To create a map, go onto the 3 dashes at the top right of your screen, and press on 'map maker'.

-Similarly, to see a replay of a match you played, press on the 3 dashes again and select 'battle log'.

-Again, to see pros play, select the 3 dashes button and then the 'brawl TV' button.

On the bottom left of your screen, there is another long rectangular button. If you press on it you will go onto your your brawl pass rewards page. On the top of the screen, however there is a button labeled 'quests'. Every time you complete a quest you will get tokens which will fill up your brawl pass and give you rewards. To get the special brawl pass, press on the golden ticket and then press on the '169 Gems' button. Then you will receive the special brawl pass.
